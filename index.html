Shikai is a Sri Lankan-born, Adelaide-based producer and DJ. His music carries a melancholic streak, weaving deep, emotive electronic textures into folk and eastern foundations, with a current focus on organic house. Since debuting in 2021 on Armada Music, he has released with Cafe De Anatolia, Soundteller Records and other great record labels, and played across Sri Lanka and Australia, in search of balance through grooves.
